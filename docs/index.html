How to turn one piece of content into ten
✏ Open with a specific campaign, launch,…
We spent $8K promoting a long-form report. 200 downloads. Painful. The content was good. The distribution was lazy. Same research. We chopped it into 11 assets over 6 weeks — LinkedIn carousels, a cold email sequence, a 90-second Reel, three newsletter blurbs, a webinar hook. Total extra spend: $0. That campaign drove 4x the pipeline of the original launch. Here's the actual breakdown of one piece → ten: 1 long-form piece (report, essay, case study) → 3 short social posts pulling one stat each → 1 carousel (the framework or the findings) → 1 email to your list (the counterintuitive angle) → 1 short video (your hottest take from it) → 1 sales enablement slide → 1 FAQ thread or comment reply bank → 1 podcast talking point → 1 retargeting ad pulling the sharpest quote → 1 follow-up post 30 days later ("what we got wrong") Most teams publish once and move on. The teams that win publish once and mine it for 60 days. Distribution isn't a bonus step. It is the strategy. What's the longest you've ever stretched a single piece of content — and what format surprised you most? #ContentMarketing #GrowthMarketing #MarketingStrategy #FounderMarketing #B2BMarketing
