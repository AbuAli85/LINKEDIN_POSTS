### 🔴 THIS WEEK'S PROBLEM: The 11pm WPS panic

It happened again last Thursday.

An HR manager at a 60-person company in Muscat sent their WPS file to the bank at 11:47pm. Payday was Friday.

The bank returned it at 12:03am. Wrong IBAN format. Employee records hadn't been updated after a bank switch three months ago.

She spent until 2:30am rebuilding the file manually, reformatting 60 rows, cross-referencing against handwritten notes. Submitted again at 2:48am.

It cleared. Salaries landed on time. Nobody knew.

Except her. And now me.

**What this actually costs:**
- 3 hours of a professional's time at midnight
- One month of stress absorbed silently
- Zero audit trail for the Ministry of Manpower
- The exact same thing will happen next month unless the underlying format issue is fixed

The WPS file format in Oman has specific bank requirements. They vary slightly by bank. They're not documented anywhere accessible. Most HR managers learn them by getting rejections.

**The fix:** Build validation into the process *before* submission — not after. SmartPRO Hub flags IBAN format errors, expired employee IDs, and bank-specific formatting issues before the file leaves your system. The rejection never happens.
